### 1 Member Profile View (Member Profile Dashboard)
When a regular member logs in, they will clearly see 4 primary widgets/cards on their dashboard:

Total Submitted Amount (Capital Balance): The total amount of money the member has deposited to date.

Profit Wallet Balance (Dividend Balance): The total amount of profit earned by the member (which is maintained separately from the capital).

Total Amount (Net Balance): The combined sum of the Capital Balance and the Profit Wallet Balance.

Transaction History: A distinct and explicit ledger statement detailing "Funds Given" (Deposits) and "Funds Taken" (Withdrawals).

### $2 Admin Panel View (Admin Control Dashboard)
The system will display the following global metrics and tools for the Administrator:

Total Association Capital: The absolute aggregate live capital amount currently held within the cooperative fund.

Member-Wise Ratio Ledger: A comprehensive table or list displaying each member's exact ownership share percentage (%).

Profit Distribution Trigger: An administrative input field to log total net profits at the end of a project/investment cycle, paired with an automated button to distribute funds to everyone's Profit Wallets in a single click.
